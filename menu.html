Menu

Beverages

From the standard through to the spectacular, the
team at Toplum have handpicked a selection of
designed to delight the palette.

We believe in quality, and that first impressions count
– and that usually starts with a sip on one of our
Toplum specials. A creamy Butter Scotch Latte, a
Oatley Milk Chai, or a refreshing Melona or a
Brazilian Lemonade.

We have the full selection of coffees, and each can be reimagined with Oatley, coconut, or almond milk. We have tea and iced tea aplenTEA, and mocktails and juices for a bit of zesty.

Food

Breakfast

The breakfast pide is a popular choice, and those that have
mustered up an appetite often opt for the full Toplum
Breakfast – a myriad of our favourite flavours that merge all
the wide-ranging essence of the Med perfectly onto one
plate.

We’re so proud of our egg collection that we named a
section after it. Eggs & Co. presents a selection beyond the
wildest of eggspectations - with Mediterranean and Middle
Eastern classics including Turkish, truffled and scrambled to
shakshuka, Benedict and more.

Main Menu

The fresh sharing mezze platters are a cut above the rest in
this region, and the starters tantalise the tastebuds
delightfully.

Mains feature fresh pasta, salad and signature plates
including octopus, lamp cutlets and charcoaled kofta.

Our twist on the popular pide presents a selection of bread-
based dishes that incorporate foodie favourites including
burrata, truffle mushroom, spicy chermoula and more.

Desserts

Save room for our decadent desert selection.
Whether it’s our popular pistachio ice cream to lightly
cleanse the palette, or a crème catalana, pecan
baklava or classic other selections – our desserts are
made freshly on site daily.
